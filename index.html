About Alliance Trust Shipping Company

Alliance Trust SC officially began operations on July 7, 1977, with 147 team members. That night, 14 small aircraft took off from Berkshire and delivered 186 packages to 25 U.K. cities from Manchester, to Liverpool, to Cardiff, to Leeds. Though the company did not show a profit until July 1979, it soon became the premier carrier of high-priority goods in the marketplace and set the standard for the express shipping industry it established. By the 1980s, Alliance Trust SC was well established. Its growth rate was compounding at about 40 percent annually, and competitors were trying to catch up. In fiscal year 1983, it reported $500 million in revenues, making business history as one of the first company to reach that financial hallmark inside 10 years of startup without mergers or acquisitions.
